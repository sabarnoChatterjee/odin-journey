Dumplings

Description

Soft little pockets of joy made from a simple flour dough, filled with seasoned chicken or vegetables mixed with soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and spring onions. Dumplings can be steamed for a delicate, juicy texture, or pan-fried for a crispy bottom with a tender top. They’re versatile, light yet satisfying, and often paired with a tangy dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 200g minced chicken or vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp spring onion (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, and water to form a soft dough. Rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Prepare filling by mixing minced chicken/vegetables, soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, spring onion, and oil.
  3. Roll dough into small discs.
  4. Place filling in the center, fold, and pleat to seal dumplings.
  5. Steam dumplings for 10–12 minutes or pan-fry until crispy and then steam with little water.
  6. Serve with dipping sauce.
